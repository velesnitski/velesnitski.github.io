# 0024 — Strawberry Dash: cuter armour + premium hats

**Date:** 2026-07-02
**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

- **Armour redesigned to be cute, not bulky** (same spirit as the socks band in [[0018]]/[[0020]]). The full metal breastplate + body-wide gold belt + big pauldron is replaced by a **small shield-shaped chest-bib** in soft steel with a gold rim and a **little red heart gem** in the middle, plus **one dainty gold-rimmed round shoulder pad**. The dino's body/belly reads clearly around it — much lighter silhouette. Perk unchanged (start with a shield).
- **Four new premium hats** (big-money sinks that pay off the level system from [[0022]]/[[0023]]):
  - **Flower Crown** — rare, 130🍓, Lv 3 (a ring of daisies with green stems)
  - **Wizard** — epic, 260🍓, Lv 5 (purple cone, curled tip, gold stars)
  - **Top Hat** — epic, 340🍓, Lv 6 (black cylinder, red band, brim)
  - **Halo** — legendary, 500🍓, Lv 9 (a glowing floating gold ring)

## Consequences

- Cosmetic only; no gameplay change. New hats slot into the existing `HATS` list, level-gate, closet preview, and dino-showcase share card automatically.
- Gives high-level players expensive goals to save toward (top hat + halo = 840🍓 combined).
