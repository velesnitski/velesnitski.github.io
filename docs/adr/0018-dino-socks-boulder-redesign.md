# 0018 — Strawberry Dash: socks-on-legs fix + fancy boulder

**Date:** 2026-07-01
**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

- **Socks weren't on the legs** — they were clipped to a small **foot ellipse**, so they only tinted the foot pad and never read as socks. Redrew them as a **knee-high striped sock** (rounded-rect up the lower leg + foot ellipse), with a blue cuff, red stripes (clipped to the sock), a gold toe cap, and an outline. Now visibly on the legs.
- **Boulder boss redesigned** from a flat grey polygon into a **rock golem**: a gradient craggy body, light/dark **facets** (clipped), a **green moss** patch with speckles, cracks, **angry brows**, **glowing yellow-red eyes**, and a zigzag grimace. Scales for the mega variant.

## Consequences

- Cosmetic-only; no gameplay change. Boulder collision/behaviour unchanged.
